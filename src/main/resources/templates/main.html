I have a collection of gear for theatrical productions that I do not get to use as often as I would like. It is available to be borrowed by community theater productions that I wish to support.

The cost is intended to be small enough that it will not be a burden to your production, yet still be enough to help me pay for the cost and upkeep for the gear. If your production is truly running on a shoestring, check with me anyhow — I may be able to help.

Select the items you wish to borrow and indicate when you want to use them. This system will generate a letter for me with the specifics of what you want.
